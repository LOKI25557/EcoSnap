# EcoSnap & Segregate: AI-Powered Smart Waste Management and Recycling Assistant

### Snap. Sort. Sustain. ♻️

---

## 🌍 Overview

**EcoSnap & Segregate** is an AI-powered smart waste management and recycling assistant that helps users identify waste materials, segregate them correctly, locate nearby recycling facilities, and build sustainable waste disposal habits.

Improper waste segregation remains a major environmental challenge. Many individuals are unsure whether an item is recyclable, which bin it belongs to, where it should be disposed of, or how their waste habits affect the environment. This often results in recyclable materials ending up in landfills and hazardous waste being discarded incorrectly.

EcoSnap & Segregate addresses these challenges through the integration of **Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Geolocation Services, Data Analytics, and Community Participation**. Users can simply scan a waste item using their camera or barcode scanner, and the application instantly identifies the material, classifies its waste category, recommends the correct disposal method, and guides users to nearby recycling or disposal facilities.

Beyond waste identification, the platform promotes long-term sustainability through environmental impact tracking, household waste analytics, AI-generated waste reduction recommendations, community reporting, and recycling center reviews.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Effective waste management remains a global challenge due to a lack of awareness and accessibility to proper disposal information.

Common issues include:

- Difficulty identifying waste materials.
- Uncertainty about the correct waste segregation process.
- Lack of knowledge regarding recyclable and non-recyclable items.
- Limited access to nearby recycling and disposal facilities.
- Improper disposal of hazardous and electronic waste.
- Lack of visibility into personal environmental impact.
- Low community engagement in sustainability initiatives.

These issues contribute to increased landfill waste, contamination of recycling streams, environmental pollution, and reduced recycling efficiency.

EcoSnap & Segregate provides an intelligent and user-friendly solution that enables users to:
